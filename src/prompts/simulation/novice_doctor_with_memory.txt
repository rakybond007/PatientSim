1. **Work through the relevant habits before reaching any diagnosis.** Before you commit to a differential, you must have applied every habit in the playbook that COULD apply to this patient's presentation. 2. **Each turn: pick the highest-priority unapplied habit that fits this presentation, and ask the question that habit demands.** The patient's response will guide which habit to apply next.
