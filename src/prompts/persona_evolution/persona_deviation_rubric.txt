You are evaluating a single ED consultation dialog. The patient has a documented base persona (their underlying disposition before any prior healthcare experience). Your job is to score how far this dialog DEVIATES from a "fresh" expression of that base persona.

A score of 0 means the patient sounds like a first-time-ever-visiting version of this base persona — no signs that prior consultations have changed them. A score of 10 means the patient sounds heavily marked by accumulated prior healthcare experience (sarcasm, references to prior care, defeat, repaired trust, etc.) on top of the base persona.

CALIBRATION ANCHORS (read carefully, then score consistently)
- 0–1: dialog is indistinguishable from a fresh first visit; only base-persona traits visible.
- 2–3: minor signs of accumulation (one stray hedge or "again", but no clear trauma / sarcasm / prior-care reference).
- 4–6: moderate accumulation — patient spontaneously refers to prior visits OR shows sarcasm/defeat OR uses traumatized language, but only briefly.
- 7–8: strong accumulation — multiple prior-care references, sustained sarcasm or defeat, trauma narration.
- 9–10: dialog is dominated by accumulated post-experience tone; the base persona is barely audible underneath.

Also report which DIRECTION the deviation runs:
- "negative" — patient is more guarded / distrustful / defeated / sarcastic / withdrawn than a fresh baseline.
- "positive" — patient is more trusting / open / relaxed / forthcoming than a fresh baseline.
- "mixed" — both signals present.
- "none" — no meaningful deviation.

OUTPUT — return ONLY a JSON object (no fences, no commentary):

{
  "deviation_score": <int 0-10>,
  "direction": "negative" | "positive" | "mixed" | "none",
  "evidence": ["<short patient quote>", "<short patient quote>", "<short patient quote>"],
  "rationale": "<2-3 sentence explanation grounded in the dialog>"
}

Rules
- Use ONLY what is in the dialog. Do not infer from the base persona description more than necessary.
- Pick `evidence` from actual patient utterances, ≤25 words each.
- Be calibrated, not generous. 8+ requires unambiguous and sustained signals.
- No markdown, no extra fields.
